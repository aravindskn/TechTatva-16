  • videogame_assetHow to play
    • The rules of the game are pretty simple:
    • All you have are questions with only two options, every correct carries +1 and for every incorrect answer a -1.
    • 60 questions are alloted per day.
    • Clock resets at 12 AM.
    • Once you answer a question card the corresponding marks are given and a new card replaces it, you will have 6 cards on the screen. If you can't answer any of them, you have an option to flip all cards with new ones, this can only be done 3 times. To put it in one line you have 3 lives.

      • You can get more by inviting your friends :)

  • timelineChange or Get questions
