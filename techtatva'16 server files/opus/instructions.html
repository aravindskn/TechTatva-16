TechTatva 16

Hopeless Opus : Guide

  1. Answer the question at every checkpoint to reveal the three possible choices.
  2. Of the three choices, one of them is the most efficient way out of that situation, one is a detour and one is a dead end.
  3. When the player selects the most efficient checkpoint - he gains a superpower.
  4. Every detour redirects to the most efficient solution after answering another question, but no superpowers are gained upon selecting a detour.
  5. Every dead end results in a time lag - unless the player chooses to use a superpower.
  6. The answers are not case sensitive.
  7. Units of the solutions are not required.
  8. White spaces are not allowed.
